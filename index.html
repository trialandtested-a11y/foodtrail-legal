Privacy Policy

Khau Gully  ·  Last updated: May 31, 2026

Khau Gully ("we", "our", or "us") is a dish-rating app that lets you photograph, score, and discover the best dishes around you. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights over it.

1. Information We Collect

Account information

  • Display name — required for every account, including anonymous ones, and shown on your public profile and ratings.
  • Anonymous accounts — when you first launch the app you can start rating immediately with just a display name. We create an anonymous account for you behind the scenes (no email, no password). You can upgrade to a permanent account later by adding an email and a unique handle.
  • Email address — collected only if you choose to upgrade your anonymous account, sign up with email directly, or reset a password.
  • Handle — a unique public username you choose when you upgrade your account.

Content you create

  • Dish photos — uploaded when you submit a rating.
  • Ratings and scores — your 0–10 score and "would go back" answer.
  • Notes — optional text you add to a rating.
  • Trails — ranked lists of dishes you create or follow.
  • Reports and blocks — actions you take to moderate other users' content.

Location

  • Approximate (coarse) device location — used only to suggest nearby restaurants while you are actively using the app. We do not store or log your location history.

Usage data

  • Standard crash reports and error logs may be collected to help us diagnose problems. These contain no personally identifiable information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate your account (anonymous or full) and display your ratings, trails, and profile to other users.
  • To identify the dish in a photo you take (processed by Google Gemini AI — see Section 4). Your photo is sent to Google's servers for analysis and is not stored by Google beyond the duration of the request.
  • To suggest restaurants near your current location.
  • To calculate dish scores using our Bayesian scoring model.
  • To moderate user-submitted text (display name, handle, trail names, notes) and keep the app safe.
  • To protect against automated abuse (captcha challenges on signup and password reset).
  • To improve the app's quality and fix bugs.

We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising.

3. How We Store Your Information

Your account data, ratings, and photos are stored securely on Supabase infrastructure. Supabase is SOC 2 Type II certified. Photos are stored in encrypted object storage. Passwords are never stored — authentication is handled via secure tokens.

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may delete your account at any time (see Section 6).

4. Third-Party Services

Supabase (supabase.com) — database, authentication, and file storage. Governed by Supabase's Privacy Policy.

Google Gemini API (ai.google.dev) — used to identify the dish in a photo you take. Your photo is transmitted to Google's servers solely for this purpose and is not used to train models. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

OpenAI (openai.com) — text you submit (display name, handle, trail names, notes) is briefly sent to OpenAI's moderation API to check for inappropriate content. Photos are not sent to OpenAI. Governed by OpenAI's Privacy Policy.

Cloudflare Turnstile — used as a captcha on signup and password reset to block automated abuse. Cloudflare receives a token and basic request metadata; no app data is shared. Governed by Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.

Apple MapKit / Core Location — used on-device to find nearby restaurants. No location data leaves your device via this service.

5. Children's Privacy

Khau Gully is not directed at children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

6. Your Rights & Account Deletion

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate information — update your display name and home city in Profile → Edit.
  • Delete your account and all associated data — go to Profile → Delete Account in the app. No email or support request is required, and this works for anonymous accounts too.
  • Export your ratings data by contacting us at the email below.

Deletion is immediate and permanent. It removes:

  • your profile (display name, handle, home city);
  • your ratings, including scores, notes, and would-go-back answers;
  • your uploaded dish photos (deleted from storage);
  • trails you own, and your follows on other trails;
  • reports, blocks, and curator-application rows associated with your account;
  • your authentication record (email, password hash, anonymous session).

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
