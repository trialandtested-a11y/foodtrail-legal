Privacy Policy

FoodTrail  ·  Last updated: May 26, 2026

FoodTrail ("we", "our", or "us") is a dish-rating app that lets you photograph, score, and discover the best street food around you. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights over it.

1. Information We Collect

Account information

  • Email address — used to create and identify your account.
  • Username / display name — shown on your public profile and ratings.

Content you create

  • Dish photos — uploaded when you submit a rating.
  • Ratings and scores — your 0–10 score and "would go back" answer.
  • Notes — optional text you add to a rating.

Location

  • Approximate device location — used only to suggest nearby restaurants while you are actively using the app. We do not store or log your location history.

Usage data

  • Standard crash reports and error logs may be collected to help us diagnose problems. These contain no personally identifiable information.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate your account and display your ratings.
  • To identify the dish in a photo you take (processed by Google Gemini AI — see Section 4). Your photo is sent to Google's servers for analysis and is not stored by Google beyond the duration of the request.
  • To suggest restaurants near your current location.
  • To improve the app's quality and fix bugs.

We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising.

3. How We Store Your Information

Your account data, ratings, and photos are stored securely on Supabase infrastructure hosted in the United States. Supabase is SOC 2 Type II certified. Photos are stored in encrypted object storage. Passwords are never stored — authentication is handled via secure tokens.

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time (see Section 6).

4. Third-Party Services

Supabase (supabase.com) — database, authentication, and file storage. Governed by Supabase's Privacy Policy.

Google Gemini API (ai.google.dev) — used to identify the dish in a photo you take. Your photo is transmitted to Google's servers solely for this purpose and is not used to train models. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Apple MapKit / Core Location — used on-device to find nearby restaurants. No location data leaves your device via this service.

5. Children's Privacy

FoodTrail is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

6. Your Rights & Account Deletion

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate information.
  • Delete your account and all associated data — go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account in the app, or email us at publishing.apps.2026@gmail.com.
  • Export your ratings data by contacting us.

Deletion requests are processed within 30 days. Photos are removed from storage; anonymised aggregated data (e.g. restaurant-level score averages) may be retained.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact Us

Questions or requests? Email us at publishing.apps.2026@gmail.com.
